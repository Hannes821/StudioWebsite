Castellum – Warfare & Cultivation 1200 A.D.

A history-inspired RTS and castle builder set in the High Middle Ages. Build mighty fortresses, command armies, govern your realm and survive the political and military struggles of medieval Europe.

Key Features

🏰 Castle Building

Design authentic medieval castles with walls, towers, gatehouses and mighty keeps.

⚔ Siege Warfare

Storm enemy fortresses with trebuchets, battering rams and siege towers.

👑 Feudal Rule

Govern your realm through taxes, justice, popularity and loyal vassals.

🌾 Medieval Economy

Produce food, ale, weapons and luxury goods.

⛪ Church & Religion

Build churches, monasteries and magnificent cathedrals.

🔥 Dynamic Destruction

Buildings burn, collapse and can be rebuilt.
