Key Features

🏰 Castle Building

Design authentic medieval castles with walls, towers, gatehouses and keeps.

⚔ Siege Warfare

Storm enemy fortresses with trebuchets, battering rams and siege towers.

👑 Feudal Rule

Manage taxes, justice, popularity and your noble vassals.

🌾 Medieval Economy

Produce food, ale, weapons and luxury goods.

⛪ Church & Religion

Build churches, monasteries and mighty cathedrals.

🔥 Dynamic Destruction

Buildings burn, collapse and can be rebuilt.
